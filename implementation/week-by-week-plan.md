# Week-by-Week Implementation Plan

## Overview
This 6-week implementation plan will take you from concept to a fully operational office efficiency service business.

---

## WEEK 1 — Research & Office Interviews

### Objectives
- Understand real office pain points
- Validate market demand
- Gather baseline data for ROI calculations

### Action Items
**Market Research**
- [ ] Visit 5 different types of offices (SME, NGO, school, professional service, retail)
- [ ] Conduct structured interviews with office managers
- [ ] Document current workflows and pain points
- [ ] Measure time spent on various administrative tasks

**Interview Questions**
1. What administrative tasks consume most time daily?
2. How many hours per week are spent on document management?
3. What are your biggest communication challenges?
4. How do you currently handle billing and invoicing?
5. What compliance deadlines do you struggle with?
6. What would you pay to save 10 hours per week in admin time?

**Deliverables**
- Market research report
- Pain point analysis
- Time and cost analysis
- Target client profiles

## Risk Mitigation

### Common Risks
1. **Client acquisition slower than expected**
   - Mitigation: Expand outreach channels, offer trial periods

2. **Service delivery issues**
   - Mitigation: Thorough testing, quality control systems

3. **Pricing resistance**
   - Mitigation: ROI demonstrations, flexible payment terms

4. **Competition**
   - Mitigation: Focus on unique value proposition, specialized service

5. **Scaling challenges**
   - Mitigation: Standardized processes, automation focus

### Contingency Plans
- Extended pilot phase if needed
- Adjust pricing based on market feedback
- Additional training for service delivery
- Partnership opportunities for scaling
