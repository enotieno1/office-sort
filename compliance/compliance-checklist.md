# Compliance & Deadline Tracking System

## Monthly Compliance Checklist

### Business Registration Compliance
```
☐ Business License Renewal
   - Due: Annually on [Registration Date]
   - Authority: County Government
   - Cost: KSh [Amount]
   - Status: ☐ Current ☐ Expiring Soon ☐ Expired

☐ Tax Compliance Certificate
   - Due: Every 6 months
   - Authority: KRA
   - Requirements: Up-to-date tax returns
   - Status: ☐ Current ☐ Pending ☐ Overdue

☐ VAT Registration Status
   - Due: Continuous monitoring
   - Authority: KRA
   - Threshold: KSh 5M annual turnover
   - Status: ☐ Registered ☐ Below Threshold ☐ Need to Register

☐ PIN Certificate Validity
   - Due: Continuous (no expiry)
   - Authority: KRA
   - Requirements: Annual tax returns
   - Status: ☐ Active ☐ Suspended ☐ Inactive
```

### Employment Compliance
```
☐ NHIF Contributions
   - Due: Monthly by 9th
   - Authority: NHIF
   - Amount: KSh [Employee Amount] per employee
   - Status: ☐ Paid ☐ Pending ☐ Overdue

☐ NSSF Contributions
   - Due: Monthly by 15th
   - Authority: NSSF
   - Amount: 6% employee + 6% employer
   - Status: ☐ Paid ☐ Pending ☐ Overdue

☐ PAYE Remittance
   - Due: Monthly by 9th
   - Authority: KRA
   - Amount: Based on employee salaries
   - Status: ☐ Paid ☐ Pending ☐ Overdue

☐ Work Injury Benefits Act (WIBA)
   - Due: Annual renewal
   - Authority: Insurance Provider
   - Coverage: All employees
   - Status: ☐ Current ☐ Expiring ☐ Expired

☐ Employment Contracts
   - Due: For all new hires
   - Authority: Ministry of Labour
   - Requirements: Written contracts for all employees
   - Status: ☐ All Current ☐ Some Missing ☐ Need Updates
```

### Financial Compliance
```
☐ Monthly Tax Returns (VAT/PAYE)

☐ Annual Financial Statements

   - Authority: Registrar of Companies
   - Requirements: Audited statements
   - Status: ☐ Prepared ☐ In Progress ☐ Not Started

☐ Annual Tax Returns
   - Due: 30th June
   - Authority: KRA
   - Method: iTax portal
   - Status: ☐ Filed ☐ Pending ☐ Overdue

☐ Bank Account Compliance
   - Due: Continuous
   - Authority: Central Bank
   - Requirements: KYC documentation
   - Status: ☐ Compliant ☐ Needs Update ☐ Non-Compliant
```

## Automated Alert System

### Google Calendar Setup
```
Alert Configuration:
- 30 days before: "Compliance Due Soon" (Low Priority)
- 14 days before: "Compliance Approaching" (Medium Priority)
- 7 days before: "Compliance Urgent" (High Priority)
- 1 day before: "Compliance Critical" (Critical Priority)
- On due date: "Compliance Due Today" (Emergency)
```

### Email Notification Templates
```
30-Day Notice:
Subject: Compliance Due Soon - [Compliance Item]
Body: This is a 30-day notice that [compliance item] is due on [due date]. Please prepare the necessary documentation and payments.

7-Day Notice:
Subject: URGENT: Compliance Due in 7 Days - [Compliance Item]
Body: URGENT: [compliance item] is due in 7 days on [due date]. Immediate action required to avoid penalties.

1-Day Notice:
Subject: CRITICAL: Compliance Due Tomorrow - [Compliance Item]
Body: CRITICAL: [compliance item] is due tomorrow on [due date]. This must be completed today to avoid penalties.
```

### WhatsApp Reminder Templates
```
Standard Reminder:
"Hi [Name], reminder that [compliance item] is due on [due date]. Status: [current status]. Reply DONE when completed."

Urgent Reminder:
"URGENT: [compliance item] is due in [days] days! Please complete immediately to avoid penalties. Call +254 XXX XXX XXX if you need help."
```

## Risk Management

### Compliance Risk Assessment
```
High Risk Items:
- Tax compliance (heavy penalties)
- Employee statutory contributions (legal action)
- Business license (closure risk)
- Safety compliance (injury liability)

Medium Risk Items:
- Environmental compliance (fines)
- Insurance coverage (business interruption)
- Financial reporting (regulatory action)

Low Risk Items:
- Documentation updates (minor penalties)
- Reporting deadlines (small fines)
```

### Mitigation Strategies
```
Prevention:
- Automated reminders and alerts
- Regular compliance reviews
- Professional consultation
- Staff training and awareness

Detection:
- Monthly compliance audits
- External compliance reviews
- Continuous monitoring
- Regular reporting

Response:
- Immediate action plans
- Professional assistance
- Penalty negotiation
- Process improvement
```
