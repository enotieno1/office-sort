## Risk Analysis

### Financial Risks
1. **Client Acquisition Slower Than Expected**
   - Impact: Delayed profitability
   - Mitigation: Increase marketing spend, referral programs

2. **Higher Than Expected Costs**
   - Impact: Reduced margins
   - Mitigation: Strict cost controls, volume discounts

3. **Client Churn Higher Than Expected**
   - Impact: Revenue instability
   - Mitigation: Excellent service, longer contracts

### Sensitivity Analysis
**Best Case**: 20% higher revenue, 10% lower costs = 50% net margin
**Base Case**: As projected = 35% net margin
**Worst Case**: 20% lower revenue, 10% higher costs = 15% net margin
