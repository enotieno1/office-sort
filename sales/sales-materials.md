# Sales & Marketing Materials

## Sales Script

**Opening**: "We help offices reduce paperwork, speed up operations, and eliminate wasted time. We handle admin, documents, billing, and compliance so your staff focus on real work."

**Key Questions**:
1. How many hours per week does your team spend on administrative tasks?
2. What are your biggest operational bottlenecks?
3. How much would it be worth to save 20+ hours per week?

**Value Proposition**: "Our clients typically save 40-60% of admin time within the first month, paying for our service 3x over."

## Marketing Messages

**Email Subject Lines**:
- "Save 20+ hours/week on office admin"
- "Transform your office efficiency"
- "Stop wasting time on paperwork"

**Social Media Posts**:
- "Is admin work killing your productivity? We can save you 50% of admin time."
- "From chaos to organized: How we transformed 5 offices this month"
- "Your competition is focused on growth. Are you still stuck in paperwork?"

## Objection Handling

**"Too expensive"**: "Our clients typically see 300% ROI within 6 months. When can we show you the math?"

**"Can do it ourselves"**: "You can, but our clients find it takes 12-18 months vs our 4-week implementation."

**"Not sure it will work"**: "We guarantee results or you don't pay. Let's start with a pilot?"
