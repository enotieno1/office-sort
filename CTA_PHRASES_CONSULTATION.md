## üéØ **MEASUREMENT SUCCESS**

### **üìä Key Metrics**

#### **Conversion Metrics:**
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR):** Percentage of clicks vs. impressions
- **Conversion Rate:** Percentage of consultations booked vs. clicks
- **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA):** Marketing spend per consultation
- **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS):** Revenue generated vs. ad spend
- **Lead Quality:** Percentage of qualified consultations

#### **Engagement Metrics:**
- **Time on Page:** Engagement with CTA content
- **Scroll Depth:** How far users read before clicking
- **Page Views:** Multiple visits before conversion
- **Session Duration:** Total time spent with CTAs
- **Interaction Rate:** Hover and engagement with CTA elements

#### **Business Impact:**
- **Consultation Bookings:** Total number of scheduled sessions
- **Show-Up Rate:** Percentage of completed consultations
- **Conversion to Sale:** Percentage becoming clients
- **Average Deal Size:** Revenue from consultation conversions
- **Customer Lifetime Value:** Long-term revenue impact
