6-Week Implementation Timeline

Transform your office operations efficiency solution into a thriving business with our structured 6-week implementation plan. Each week builds upon the previous, ensuring a solid foundation for sustainable growth.

1

Week 1: Research & Office Interviews

Primary Objectives

  • Visit 5 local offices to understand pain points
  • Document current administrative challenges
  • Measure time spent on various admin tasks
  • Identify most pressing needs

Key Activities

  • Prepare interview questionnaire
  • Schedule office visits
  • Conduct structured interviews
  • Document findings and observations
  • Analyze common patterns

Expected Outcomes

  • Comprehensive market research data
  • Validated problem statements
  • 5 potential pilot clients identified
  • Clear understanding of market needs

Tools Needed

  • Interview templates
  • Data collection sheets
  • Recording device (optional)
  • Analysis spreadsheet
